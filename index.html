360FX is composed of a team of people with a unique approach to table top and special effects.  The constant hunt to find new, innovative and breakthrough way of showing products has permitted the creation of our own specific machine allowing to stay in focus even when in camera movement; a track in/out, a tilt in/out and even a panning which remains a challenge when using high speed.  A static shot is replaced by a smooth camera movement whilst keeping the product in focus giving it a stronger effect.

ROCCO: THE MOTION CONTROL ROBOT

The revolutionary last generation high speed motion control ensures extraordinary performances to realize unbelievable and breathtaking frames.
This unique robot is one of the few examples in Europe and promises to revolutionize the world of table top broadening its horizons.
Rocco has a highly precise articulated arm with 6 degrees of freedom for optimum flexibility.

A unique spherical work envelope allows maximum utilization of cell workspace. Additional benefits include floor and ceiling mount options for easy robot integration. The robot arm’s rugged, fully enclosed structure (rated IP65) makes it ideal for a wide range of applications, even in harsh environments.
